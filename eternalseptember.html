We are opening up the campus to individual stays for Eternal September.

Eternal September is like a combination of a rationalist community coworking space, an inn, a large hamlet, a very small city, and a month-long unconference.

There is a calendar. It runs from September 16th to October 13th. (Yes, half of Eternal September is October.) If you purchace access, you can attend sessions on this calendar. You can also add sessions to it. What sort of session is largely up to you and your fellow campus occupants.

There are two ways to purchace access to Eternal September:

  • Rooms starting from $60 per night
  • Coworking access at $30 per day

Both of these have a 10% discount per week purchased, (eg, if you buy four weeks of common you will get 40% off of your total price).

If you would like to know some facts about what it would be like to come to Eternal September, please read our FAQ below. If you would like to know more about what the general vibe will be like, keep reading.

FAQ

Who will be there?

People who purchase access.

What sorts of sessions will people run? What sorts of sessions can I run?

You can run anything you'd like. One member of the lightcone team, Ronny Fernandez, will likely run authentic relating games and sing alongs.

Can I invite my team?

Yes. Depending on your team size you may want to rent a larger office. (Each additional person should also pay for general access, but you only pay once for the office)

Will Lightcone team be running a bunch of programming for this?

Maybe some, but mostly we'll be focused on maintaining basic infrastructure for the campus (and for LessWrong). But, over the past few months we've found the people organically staying here to have a good time – sometimes hosting events on their own, sometimes just enjoying quiet spaciousness.

I already live in Berkeley. Is there a point to coming?

Well, the depends on you and what you find valuable! But, here are reasons you might want to come:

  • Experiment with working on the spacious Lighthaven campus.
  • Try out living in our soundproof, lightproof bedrooms and see how you like it.
  • Come for a mini vacation. Take a break from your usual life.
  • Visit with your significant other for a romantic gateway.
  • Host social events at Lighthaven (discussions, impromptu workshops, games and dances)

Can I bring guests for a day?

If you've paid for at least a week of space-access, you can invite people over for the day. (If you want to bring them over multiple times, check in with us first)

Can I bring my family?

Yes. We'll be working to make sure there's space where children can hang out, and I expect we can find you a cluster of bedrooms that meet your needs. (Each additional person should also pay for general access. We are figuring out pricing for children, most likely ~50% of general access)

What exactly is included?

Snacks are included with a room and with common space access. There are no restrictions on where you can go, other than other people's private rooms/offices, unless they invite you.
